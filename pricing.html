Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really a free tier?

Yes. Every tool works with a free license key — no credit card needed. Free tier gives you 50 checks/day per tool (1,500/month), which covers most local development and hobby use.

Can I buy just one tool instead of the suite?

Absolutely. If you only need API Contract Guardian, pay $19/mo. Need both API checking and SQL conversion? Buy both for $28/mo — or grab the suite at $39/mo and save on the rest. License keys are per-tool or all-tools.

How does licensing work?

Set the REVENUEHOLDINGS_LICENSE_KEY environment variable. The key is a JWT that encodes your tier and tool access. Works offline for up to 7 days between automatic verifications. No account or login required.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit/debit cards via Stripe. Annual billing available at ~17% discount. Enterprise invoices and purchase orders supported.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — no lock-in contracts. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period. After that you revert to the free tier with no data loss.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Not yet — but the free tier gives you full CLI access with a daily rate limit. You can evaluate every tool's complete feature set on the free tier before upgrading. When Pro launches, we'll offer a 14-day trial with no credit card required.

What happens if I exceed the free tier limits?

Your CLI tools continue to work — you'll see a notice that you've hit your daily rate limit and the check will be queued for the next day. No data is lost, no features are disabled. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited usage.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period. You keep access to all your data and configurations regardless of plan changes.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked. Contact us at support@revenueholdings.dev and we'll process it within 48 hours.

Do you offer student or open-source discounts?

Yes. The free tier covers open-source projects with 50 checks/day per tool. For active open-source maintainers, we offer free Pro access — email us at oss@revenueholdings.dev.
